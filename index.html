Global Country Information 2023

Comprehensive global dataset offering a diverse range of indicators for every country. Includes demographics, economics, environment, healthcare, education, and more. Enables in-depth analysis and cross-country comparisons.

The CPI values provide insights into the perceived levels of corruption, with darker shades indicating higher levels of corruption and lighter shades representing lower levels. This visual representation offers a clear and comparative view of the global landscape of corruption perception.

Each bar denotes a different nation, with its length directly proportional to the IMR. Shorter bars signify lower rates of infant mortality, indicating superior healthcare and living conditions. This visualization allows for a direct comparison of infant mortality rates, offering crucial insights into global healthcare outcomes.

Land area is on the horizontal axis (sq. km), while CO2 emissions are on the vertical axis. Each bubble's size represents the population of the respective country, providing a visual insight into its demographic scale. This visualization allows for a deep examination of the interplay between land use, CO2 emissions, and population across nations.

The line chart depicts the relationship between a country's Consumer Price Index (CPI) and time. CPI measures the average change in prices over time for a basket of goods and services, reflecting inflation or deflation. This chart helps in understanding the purchasing power and economic stability of a country over a specific period.










The line chart illustrates the trend of a country's Gross Domestic Product (GDP) per million units of currency over time. This metric represents the economic output per million of the country's currency units, providing insight into its economic performance and productivity on a per capita basis.
